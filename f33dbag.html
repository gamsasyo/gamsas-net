// what it is

F33DBAG turns your phone into a little instrument panel. pick a look, ride the faders, shoot. the look bakes straight into the photo and the video — what you see is what you get, nothing changes on the way out.

every look is a metal shader we wrote by hand. the science is borrowed from apple — vision, core ml, the camera stack — the art is the part we make ourselves. that's the whole studio, really.

// a few of the looks

EMBERsoak it in one color — color, level, bloom
